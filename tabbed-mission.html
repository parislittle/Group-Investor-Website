Our Mission


Drink Spiking is our Problem

Our Problem

Drink spiking in colleges across America is a serious and pervasive problem that puts students' safety and well-being at risk. Each year, countless students fall victim to this disturbing practice, which often goes unreported and leaves lasting physical and emotional trauma. It is time for us to take action and create a culture of awareness, prevention, and support for all those affected by this issue.

Our Solution

We have took this problem personally with our team. Too many friends and family members either know someone who has expereienced drink spiking or has expreienced drink spiking first hand. We aim to change this through a simple and innovate solution, TABBED. TABBED is an ingenious and deceptively simple can design that requires no external objects, using the lid as a covering for your drink. All you gotta do is pop it open and turn the tab around when your not drinking, and enojy 100% worry free.

Our Ask

To stop this pervasive issue our ask is for each and every Individual to take the issue of drink spiking personally. Keep vigilant at parties, talk to your friends, and take care of those in need. If you're ready to take this mission, we ask that you invest your time and money to change the colleg drinking scene one drink at a time.
