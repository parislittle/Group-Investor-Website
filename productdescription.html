In-Depth Product Description



Tabbed is an innovative product that provides a simple solution to the problem of drink spiking. It is a can of a popular beverage, but with a twist. Each can has an aluminum tab that covers the drinking hole. When you want to take a sip, you simply flip the tab open and enjoy your drink. The tab snaps back into place once you are finished, ensuring that your drink remains secure from any potential spikers.

But Tabbed is not just about functionality; we also wanted to make sure that the product is enjoyable to use. That's why we offer a range of delicious flavors, including mango mosaic, grapefruit gusto and citrus symphony, to satisfy any taste buds. Our drinks are also made with natural ingredients and contain no artificial sweeteners or preservatives, so you can feel good about what you're putting in your body.

At Tabbed, our primary focus is on college campuses. We know that these environments can be particularly risky when it comes to drink spiking, which is why we have made it our mission to provide a safe and enjoyable experience for college students everywhere. Our product is perfect for parties, social gatherings, and other events where drinks are being served. With Tabbed, you can enjoy your night out with friends without worrying about the dangers of drink spiking.

One of the unique features of Tabbed is the aluminum tab itself. It is designed to be strong and durable, so you can be sure that it will remain in place and protect your drink from spiking attempts. The tab is also recyclable, so you can feel good about using a product that is environmentally friendly.

Another benefit of Tabbed is that it is incredibly easy to use. Simply pop open the tab to take a sip, and snap it back into place when you're done. There's no need to worry about complicated mechanisms or special instructions – Tabbed is designed to be intuitive and user-friendly.

We believe that Tabbed has the potential to revolutionize the way we think about drink safety. By providing a simple and effective solution to the problem of drink spiking, we hope to make a real difference in the lives of people everywhere. Our product is already generating a buzz on college campuses across the country, and we're excited to see where it goes from here.

In addition to providing a great product, we are also committed to giving back to the community. A portion of the profits from each can of Tabbed sold goes to organizations that work to prevent drink spiking and support victims of this type of crime. We believe that everyone deserves to feel safe and protected when they go out, and we're proud to be doing our part to make that a reality.

Overall, Tabbed is an exciting new product that offers a simple, effective, and enjoyable solution to the problem of drink spiking. With its range of delicious flavors, durable aluminum tab, and user-friendly design, it's no wonder that Tabbed is already becoming a favorite among college students and party-goers everywhere. We're thrilled to be bringing this innovative product to market, and we look forward to seeing the positive impact it will have on communities around the world.
