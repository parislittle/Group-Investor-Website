ABOUT US

We are a team of college students excited about cultivating a safer drinking culture. We started Tabbed because we noticed the prevalence of drink spiking across college campuses. Our quest for a solution to prevents drink spiking led us to Tabbed: a carefully crafted drink with a re-designed lid.

Introducing "Tabbed" - the ultimate solution to prevent drink spiking and ensure a safer party culture! Developed by a group of forward-thinking college students turned entrepreneurs, this innovative product is a game-changer in the fight against drink tampering. With its discreet and easy-to-use design, Tabbed is a small device that can be attached to any drink container. It detects common date rape drugs and alerts the user in real-time, providing a crucial tool to protect against potential harm. Say goodbye to worries about drink safety and hello to a fun and safe party experience with Tabbed - the must-have product for any college student looking to take control of their own safety. Join us in our mission to empower and protect young adults with this revolutionary product!
